For this Guided Practice assignment, you will be assisting with the redesign of the company website, Trusted Friends Daycare.

 Many of their clients access the website from their mobile devices, therefore they are hoping to improve the website appearance on these devices
  to include not just phones, but also tablets and desktop computers.   

In addition, the site contains several pages that clients will need to print, so your design will need to meet the needs of printed media as well.
